Pricing FAQ

What does “free during beta” really mean?

Every tier costs Rp 0 until we exit closed beta. We'll email all beta users at least 30 days before any billing starts — no surprise charges, no quiet auto-conversion.

Will current beta users get a discount?

Yes. Beta participants get founding-family pricing locked in for the first year after we exit beta — lower than the rates shown above.

Why publish prices if billing isn't on yet?

Transparency, and so payment partners can review our business model during onboarding. The prices above are what we're building toward.

Which payment methods will you accept?

Credit and debit cards globally; QRIS, GoPay, OVO, and bank transfer in Indonesia via local payment gateways.
