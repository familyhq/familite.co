We believe the "Mental Load" shouldn't be a solo journey.

"familite.co was born from a simple realization: modern life is distributed, but our family information is fragmented. We didn't need another app; we needed one place for it all."

The Problem We're Solving

Most families manage their lives through a chaotic mix of WhatsApp threads, sticky notes, and private calendar entries. This creates an invisible burden—the Mental Load—that usually falls on one person.

For families living across timezones or managing multiple household locations, this chaos is amplified. Whether it's "What time is the flight in my time?" or "Where is the gate key code?", the constant back-and-forth drains our most precious resource: family time.

Our Core Philosophy

A Single Source of Truth

familite.co is designed as a collaborative digital handbook. It’s one shared space where money, dates, and routines live together—accessible to everyone who needs them, whenever they need them.

Storage-Agnostic Freedom

We don't want to lock your data in a proprietary silo. Whether you use Google Drive, iCloud, or Dropbox, familite.co acts as the universal reference layer that connects your family to your existing documents.

Privacy as a Right

Family life is private. We use industry-standard security and granular role-based access to ensure that sensitive data like bank balances are only seen by the right people.

Built for Every Member

The Organizer

The "Household COO." You're the one who keeps the wheels turning, uploads the bills, and sets the reminders. We give you the automation (like PDF parsing) to stop the manual admin and start leading.

The Participant

The family member who just needs the answers. No more texting for the WiFi password or the nanny's contact. Get what you need in two taps without asking for help.

Be a Founding Family.

We are currently in a closed beta, working with our first 50 families to shape the future of household management. We'd love for yours to be one of them.
