Upload bank statements and let Familite do the work. You stay in control with a simple verification wizard.

Bills

Never miss a due date again.

One inbox for every household bill — utilities, subscriptions, school fees, insurance. Forward, upload, or paste; we draft the bill, you approve, the family stays informed.

  • Bill Inbox

    Drop in PDFs or forward emails to your family's inbox address. Familite parses amount, due date, and payee into a draft bill for review.

  • Track & Stay Ahead

    Mark paid, snooze, or set a due date. Everyone sees what's outstanding at a glance, no chasing required.

  • Shared Visibility

    Bills surface alongside events and todos in Planning, so the household sees what's coming due without asking.
